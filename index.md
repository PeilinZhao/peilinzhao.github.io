## BIO
Peilin Zhao is currently a professor at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. He previously worked at Tencent Group, Ant Group, A*STAR Singapore, and Rutgers University. His research focuses on machine learning and its applications, including large models, AI for science, AI for games, and recommendation systems. He has served as an Area Chair and Associate Editor for top-tier venues such as ICML, NeurIPS, and TPAMI.
